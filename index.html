Theoretical Solid-State Physics



Welcome to the project of phonon visualization provided by the Theoretical Solid State Physics group of the University of Luxembourg.
This project provides the opportunity to communicate recent research results of the theoretical solid-state physics group and by its interactivity it gives to users a better understanding of the physics of different materials. Thereby, the main focus lies on visualizing phonons (lattice vibrations) of these materials by an interplay between their phonon dispersion and the corresponding animated states of these systems. So in addition to phonon dispersion curves, the database provides a multitude of animated crystal structures and further informations about the materials.
